# Watershed Watch

The first **vertical wedge** — a basin-scoped environmental deadline-and-action radar for volunteer
conservation coalitions. Thick vertical, **selective reuse** of DocketClock.

> Fit verdict: **builds on DocketClock partially.** It RENTS reconciled federal windows + the
> confidence model + OCD-IDs, and OWNS the three things the bare registry can't: EPA EIS ingestion +
> its 45/30-day clock, HUC geo-recall, and the monitor→act→receipt loop. That ownership is what
> keeps it from being "just a Waterkeeper skin."

**Full design + fit analysis:** [`docs/architecture/watershed-watch.md`](../../docs/architecture/watershed-watch.md)

Before committing a partner, run the **falsifiable anti-skin test** and W1–W3 spikes
([plan](../../docs/plans/week1-validation-spikes.md)): is `POST /comments` open? is EPA EIS
machine-readable? are there enough novel in-basin windows/quarter to justify a paid seat?

First partner: a **staffed** Chesapeake Bay org (CBF regional office or funded Waterkeeper affiliate)
the user can reach through their network — so the design partner IS the paying customer.

Status: **stub.** Gated behind a committed partner + the Week-1 spikes.
